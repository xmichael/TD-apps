Land Use Comparison

These maps allow users to get an impression of how agriculture has changed in the area over the last 200 years.

1840s

For taxation purposes, information on land use was collected as part of the Welsh tithe maps survey. The project digitised the 1840’s field boundaries in order to be able to display land use by area. Note that land use at the level of individual fields was not recorded in every parish, and that in 6 of the 12 parishes in the Dyfi Biosphere area; where possible, historical land use has been inferred.

1930s

Outputs from the first Land Use Utilisation Survey, as instigated by L. Dudley Stamp.

Contemporary Land Use

Data from Environment Systems Ltd using a combination of National Forest Inventory data, OpenStreetMap and multi-temporal satellite imagery

Cymharu Defnydd Tir

Mae'r mapiau hyn yn caniatáu i ddefnyddwyr gael argraff o sut mae amaethyddiaeth wedi newid yn yr ardal dros y 200 mlynedd diwethaf.

1840au

At ddibenion trethiant, casglwyd gwybodaeth am ddefnydd tir fel rhan o arolwg mapiau degwm Cymru. Fe wnaeth y prosiect ddigideiddio ffiniau caeau'r 1840’au er mwyn gallu arddangos defnydd tir yn ôl ardal. Sylwch na chofnodwyd defnydd tir ar lefel caeau unigol ym mhob plwyf, ac mewn 6 o'r 12 plwyf yn ardal Biosffer Dyfi; lle bo hynny'n bosibl, casglwyd defnydd tir hanesyddol.

1930au

Allbynnau o'r Arolwg Defnyddio Defnydd Tir cyntaf, fel y cychwynnwyd gan L. Dudley Stamp.
